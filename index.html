Gurnam Bhullar Fan Page

Biography

Gurnam Bhullar is a renowned Punjabi singer and actor known for his melodious voice and versatile acting skills. Born on February 8, 1995, in Mansa, Punjab, India, he started his career in the music industry and later ventured into acting.

Source: Wikipedia

Music Career

Gurnam Bhullar gained recognition for his hit songs, including "Diamond Di Jhanjhar," "Phone Maar Di," and "Jinna Tera Main Kardi." His soulful Punjabi music has captivated audiences worldwide.

Awards and Recognition

Gurnam Bhullar has received several awards for his contributions to Punjabi music and cinema. He won the Best Debut Male Award at the PTC Punjabi Film Awards for his performance in the film "Guddiyan Patole."
